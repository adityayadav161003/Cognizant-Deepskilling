Expression: 10 + 5 * 2
Result: 20
Explanation: '*' has higher precedence than '+'. So, 5 * 2 = 10, then 10 + 10 = 20.

Expression: (10 + 5) * 2
Result: 30
Explanation: Parentheses '()' have the highest precedence. So, (10 + 5) = 15, then 15 * 2 = 30.

Expression: 100 - 20 / 4 * 3 + 15 % 4
Result: 88
Explanation:
1. '/', '*', and '%' have equal precedence and are evaluated left-to-right (associativity):
   - 20 / 4 = 5
   - 5 * 3 = 15
   - 15 % 4 = 3
2. The expression simplifies to: 100 - 15 + 3
3. '-' and '+' have equal precedence, evaluated left-to-right:
   - 100 - 15 = 85
   - 85 + 3 = 88
